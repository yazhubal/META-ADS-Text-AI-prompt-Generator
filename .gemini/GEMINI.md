## Copywriting Principles for META
- **The "3-Second Rule":** Hooks must capture attention immediately before the user scrolls past.
- **The "Punchy First 125":** Ensuring the primary value proposition is visible before the "See More" cutoff on mobile.
- **Direct & Persuasive:** Avoiding "corporate talk" and fluff. Using emotional triggers and power words that resonate with specific sub-cultures (e.g., bikers, hobbyists).
- **Clear CTA:** Every ad must have a singular, unmistakable call to action.
